Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Good IPTV Player (the “Application”), developed by NESIMI UMUTCAN OZTURK (the “Service Provider”), handles information. The Application is an advertising-supported media player. It does not provide playlists, channels, subscriptions, or media content.

No account and no developer-operated content server

The Application does not require an account with the Service Provider. The Service Provider does not operate a server that receives your playlists, Xtream credentials, viewing history, favorites, or parental-control PIN.

Information stored on your device

Playlist names and URLs, Xtream usernames and passwords, favorites, categories, settings, and parental-control PINs are stored locally on your device so the Application can provide its features. This local data remains until you delete a playlist, reset app data, or uninstall the Application.

This locally stored information is not currently encrypted by the Application. Protect access to your device. If you enter an HTTP address rather than HTTPS, data sent to that provider may travel without transport encryption.

Connections to services you choose

The Application connects directly from your device to playlist and streaming servers that you enter. Those third-party providers receive the network information needed to answer the request, which may include your IP address and any credentials included in the request. Their privacy practices are controlled by them, not by the Service Provider.

If you choose an external player such as VLC or MX Player, the Application passes the selected stream URL to that app. The external player’s privacy policy then applies.

Advertising and automatically processed data

The Application uses the Google Mobile Ads SDK (AdMob). Google states that this SDK may automatically collect and share IP address (which can be used to estimate general location), app interactions, diagnostic information, and device or account identifiers for advertising, analytics, and fraud-prevention purposes. This data is encrypted in transit by Google.

The Application requests non-personalized ads. It does not request precise-location permission and does not use precise location. Where required, Google’s consent form is used before ads are requested and provides access to applicable privacy choices. Google may retain information under its own policies.

Google privacy information: Google Privacy Policy and AdMob privacy information.

Artificial intelligence

The Application does not use artificial intelligence to process your information or provide recommendations.

Children

The Application is not directed to children under 13, and the Service Provider does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The Application does not provide or control the content a user adds.

Your choices

You can delete individual playlists or use Reset App Data in Settings to erase data stored by the Application. Where Google’s privacy options are required, they are available from Settings. You can also use your operating system’s advertising controls or uninstall the Application.

Security

Reasonable measures are used to limit unnecessary data handling, but no device, network, or storage method is completely secure. Use HTTPS providers when available and do not share playlist credentials with people you do not trust.

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated when the Application or legal requirements change. The effective date above will be revised when an update is published.

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact nesimiztrk@gmail.com.
